Trust & limits

Lawbot helps you draft and organize using retrieved court text when available. It is not a lawyer and not your law firm.

  • Verify cites — Match every statute, rule, and case citation to official sources before filing.
  • Clerk rules — Formatting and forms change by court; confirm locally.
  • AI can be wrong — Even when quotes match loaded text, strategy and law can still be incomplete or mistaken.
  • Privileged material — Do not paste confidential client information you are not allowed to share with the service.
